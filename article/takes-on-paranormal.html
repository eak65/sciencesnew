Influencer takes on the paranormal

Skeptic teaches critical reasoning while addressing paranormal claims.

Share this:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Pocket
  • Reddit
  • Print
  • Email

By Suato Gupka

March 21, 2021 at 3:02 pm

An entreprenuer and scientist is using his platform to teach scientific skeptism. Ethan Keiser believes it's extremely important to teach science in a exciting way. "We must meet Gen Z on platforms they are already using", said Ethan. A study conducted by Springtide Research Institute, who chart the changes in the lives of Generation Z, found that our waning relationship with religion in its most conventional form is changing. The group surveyed over 10,000 young people between the ages of 13-25, and found that 71% of that group considered themselves at least slightly religious, but had little trust or interest in engaging with religious institutions.

Ethan explores haunted locations and live streams his adventure. "I always get permission before exploring, a lot people love the idea of debunking paranormal activity", said Ethan. With over 500K followers on the popular social platform Tiktok, he has some avid fans. "Nothing is better than getting positive messages from fans. It warms my heart when I hear that I've inspired someone to pursue a career in science."

Ethan has become so popular that he is traveling all over the world. He wishes to "triple down" on Tiktok as he believes it's the platform of the future.
